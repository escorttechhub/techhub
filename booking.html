Welcome to the Ideathon Page

What is an Ideathon?

An ideathon is a brainstorming event where participants collaborate to generate innovative ideas and solutions to specific challenges within a limited time frame. Unlike a hackathon, which focuses on developing working prototypes, an ideathon emphasizes idea generation and concept development.

How Can You Participate and Learn?

Participants can sign up individually or in teams, collaborate on guided themes, and work together to brainstorm and refine ideas. Expert mentors guide participants, and teams present their ideas to judges for evaluation.

Importance of Ideathons

Ideathons drive innovation, build communities, and serve as early-stage concept development platforms. They also encourage entrepreneurial spirit, with many ideas serving as foundations for startups or research projects.
